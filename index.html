Defining Designer

What does it really mean to be a Graphic Designer? Do we make prom posters and holiday cards or perhaps do we do something more? Make something more? Mean something more? The thing about Design that seems to baffle me is that there is no clear winner in what you have to do. It is open for interpretation and because of that your role in what is called a "Designer" may change and fluctuate. This website speaks to what my role in design is, my fears, my tips and what I find important.
